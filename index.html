TL;DR

We propose SWSformer (Subwindow Shuffle Transformer), a highly efficient and novel Transformer-based architecture for image restoration. By introducing a subwindow shuffle mechanism, our model significantly reduces computational overhead while effectively capturing both local and global dependencies, achieving state-of-the-art performance in various image restoration tasks such as denoising and deblurring.
